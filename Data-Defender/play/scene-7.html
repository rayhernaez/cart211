7.

You get a notification: Jon P. wants to add you. Their profile looks normal, with a pet photo and a short bio. It shows 12 mutual friends from school clubs. The account was made 2 weeks ago.

What do you do?

  1. Accept — the mutuals mean they're probably fine.
  2. Message “Do I know you?” and see what they say.
  3. Ignore or restrict — review your privacy settings so strangers see less.
