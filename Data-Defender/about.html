ABOUT

About the site

Data Defender is a mini training game. You'll see 8 scenarios from everyday internet life and pick what you would do. If you get most answers right, you earn a simple certificate at the end. This site is kid-friendly and does not track you.

Data Defender runs in your browser only. Each scene randomizes the choices, you tap one, and the “Next” button appears. Your progress (alias, your choices, score, and the date you finished) is saved to your own device using localStorage so the quiz can move from page to page. Your alias is just for the certificate screen and never leaves your device. When you start the quiz again, the saved data is cleared. This project does not use ads, trackers, or analytics. It loads images and sounds from the site files, and fonts from Google Fonts.

About Surveillance Capitalism

Some apps and sites make money by learning what people do online and guessing what they'll do next. This website project is not meant to scare you. It shows small and practical choices you can make. The goal is pretty simple: share less, turn on only the permissions necessary, and keep your voice your own.
